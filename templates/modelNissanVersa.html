Nissan Versa Note

Despite sharing a name with its sedan counterpart, the Versa Note hatchback is quite a different animal. Shorter front and rear overhangs give the Versa Note a sportier look and its hatchback design provides more versatility. Although it can’t match the sedan’s low price, the Versa Note is still one of the least expensive 5-doors you can buy. The Versa Note offers a fairly large back seat and cargo area, not to mention some very cool technology.
