Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG 6x6

The Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG 6×6 is a Pickup-Truck-SUV manufactured by Mercedes-Benz. A derivative of the Mercedes Geländewagen, at the time it was the company's largest and second most expensive street-legal offroad vehicle,[citation needed] manufactured by Magna Steyr in Graz, Austria. It combined the engine from the G63, a twin-turbo V-8, with a 6x6 portal axles, with pick-up version of the G-Clas body, and a luxury interior. It was produced from 2013 to 2015, with production exceeding 100 vehicles.
